About me

Who's behind the builds?

Hey! I'm Onelots, an Android enthusiast and open-source contributor. I spend most of my time in AOSP source trees — working on devices, fixing bugs, and shipping builds that just work.

I'm a Project Member & Maintainer at EvolutionX and a Maintainer at LineageOS — two of the most respected custom ROM projects out there.

All my builds are compiled by myself, tested on the devices I actually own, and maintained with long-term support in mind. No shortcuts. No device dead after 1 month.
